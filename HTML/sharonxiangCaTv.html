Concept Design: Smart TV

We try to bring new experience of navigation for smart TV users. It aims to reduce the frustration when users browse through or search specific channels. It also allows users to do social activities with their friends while watching TV shows.




Problems for usual Smart TVs

The remote that comes with the TV is usually a typical model, which has numbers and arrow buttons. The problem for this kind of navigation is that it takes too long for users to achieve the goal, and usersâ€™ frustration level can be very high due to the mistakes that might occur during the browse/input process.







Wireframes & Sketches

Since the remote design is one the problems that slows users down. We wanted to design a remote that users can directly type the terms that they already know. Also we wanted to corporate smartphones as a part of the remote.
